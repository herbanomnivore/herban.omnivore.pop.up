About Us

Herban Omnivore is a student-run pop-up home kitchen located in Berkeley, California, offering plant-forward fine-dining resembling fusion cuisine at an affordable price. Inspired by global cuisines and nostalgic Taiwanese flavors, we aim to spotlight the idea of conscious eating in a casual, budget-friendly, and accessible setting. Perfect for college students, the menu this semester encourages reflection on life experiences and future aspirations, in which you get interact with and customize your meals based on your experience. Past Menus

2024 Spring - Night Market x Dining Table

Night Market x Dining Table featured a fully plant-based menu capturing the whimsical marriage of bold street food flavors and humble gastronomical traditions in Taiwan. The menu reconciles traditions with new experiences, spotlighting nostalgic Taiwanese flavors--from night markets to our dinner tables--with an emphasis on local Californian seasonal produce and global culinary inspirations. Techniques like fermentation and ingredients like soy will be spotlighted as an origin of discourses regarding how culture plays a role in what constitutes as sustainable cuisine.

Menu: Winter 2023 - Contemplate

"Contemplate" featured a gallery of winter Californian produce through a plant-forward approach. The menu was crafted in a way that encourages guests to engage in contemplation from the natural, material, then spiritual realm. The zine features food systems related artwork along with a perspective piece about the conundrum between individual, food ethics, cultural pillars, and normative definitions of sustainble food choices.

Menu: Summer 2023 - Soil x Ocean

This 10-course menu was crafted through the lens of my hometown: Taipei. An array of farm fresh local summer produce hand-selected from traditional farmers' in Taiwan married with housemade traditional Taiwanese condimets and ferments, all presented through a global culinary narrative. Zero-waste fine dining was emphasized, in which all parts of one whole chicken and all vegetable scraps were utilized and reinvented into different dishes. Each guest was given a handrawn mini zine that highlights Taiwanese local produce and introduces the though process and stories bind each dish.

What is the menu like this semester?

The menu this semester will be designed to take you on a reflective journey of vivid memories regarding your college experience. You will get to think about, play with, and choose what you eat. There will be 5 courses and 1 to 2 drinks, either served as a cocktail or mocktail by choice. The dishes will emphasize seasonal Californian produce, therefore slightly changing according to the month. The price will be $45 per person, but please contact me below for any questions or concerns. I really want to make this experience as accessible for everyone as possible!!
